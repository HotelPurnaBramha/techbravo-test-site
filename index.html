Understanding the OwnADX JS Tag Creation

This environment is designed to test the TechBravo supply-side integration. Below is the technical breakdown of how these tags are generated in the console.

How to Create a JS Tag Endpoint [cite: 1, 2]

  • Step 1: Navigate to SSP â†’ Create Endpoint on your TechBravo dashboard[cite: 4, 5].
  • Step 2: Enter Basic Information, including a unique Partner Name and Endpoint Name (e.g., "TestPartner_Banner_300x250")[cite: 7, 10, 13].
  • Step 3: Select JS Tag as the Integration Type[cite: 17, 20].
  • Step 4: Configure the Ad Format (e.g., JS Banner) and target Device Type (e.g., Desktop)[cite: 29, 32, 39, 42].
  • Step 5: Set your Revenue Share and Default Bid Floor. These values are critical for mapping and bidding behavior[cite: 60, 68, 79].
  • Step 6: Choose the Ad Size from the IAB standards or enter custom dimensions[cite: 81, 83, 86].

How to Copy the Tag [cite: 115, 117]

Once you have verified all details and mapped your DSP RTB endpoints, click Save/Create Endpoint. To retrieve the tag for implementation:

  • Locate your endpoint in the DSP/SSP list[cite: 118].
  • Click the WiFi Sign (broadcast icon) to open the tag details window[cite: 118].
  • Copy the generated <script> block and share it with your partner or place it in these repositories[cite: 131, 134].
